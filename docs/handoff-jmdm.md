# jmdm.studio — editing guide (for Jack)

Your site is live at **https://jmdm.studio**, and you edit it yourself — no code, no deploys.

## Sign in

1. Go to **https://dubbyynbuy5nm.cloudfront.net** (bookmark it).
2. Click **Sign in with Google** and use your Google account (`jack.dalgleishmorel@gmail.com`).
3. You land in the editor, with a **live preview** of your site on the right.

## The editor

- The left side is your content as **blocks** (cards), grouped by page — the tabs at the top:
  **Index of works** (`/`), **About** (`/about`), **Shop** (`/shop`).
- Each block is a small form. Type, and the preview on the right updates as you go.
- **Index of works** — your catalogue. Add / reorder / delete works; each has a title, summary,
  description, tags, and images.
- **About** — your bio, contact details, the two CV lists (selected works, press / writing), and the
  "currently" cards.
- **Shop** — items (in **beta**, hidden from the public until checkout is set up).
- **Add a block:** the `+ …` buttons at the bottom of a page.
- **Reorder:** the ↑ ↓ arrows on a row. **Delete:** the ×.

## Adding photos

- In a work's **Image URLs** field, click **upload image** and pick a photo. It uploads and appears
  in the preview. Add as many as you like per work.
- Until you add real photos, the site shows the striped/placeholder swatches by design.

## Saving

- Click **Save** (top right). Your changes go live on **jmdm.studio** immediately — the public site
  reads your content directly.

## Your public site

- **https://jmdm.studio** — what visitors see.

## Good to know

- The **shop is in beta** (hidden from visitors) until online checkout is added later.
- Only your allow-listed Google account can sign in. If you ever can't log in, that's the first thing
  to check.
